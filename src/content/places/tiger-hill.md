`Tiger Hill` often is the first Suzhou expansion that makes sense after the core city already is secure.

## The short answer

It is usually worth it when:

- the stay is an overnight or fuller version
- the route wants a broader landmark block
- you would rather vary the city than add another similar garden

It is less worth forcing when:

- Suzhou only has one compressed day
- [Humble Administrator's Garden](/places/humble-administrators-garden/) and [Pingjiang Road](/places/pingjiang-road/) still are not secure

## Tiger Hill vs another garden

Choose `Tiger Hill` if:

- you want a different kind of Suzhou half day
- the city already has enough delicate garden texture

Choose [Lingering Garden](/places/lingering-garden/) or [Humble Administrator's Garden](/places/humble-administrators-garden/) if:

- your first priority still is Suzhou's classical-garden identity
