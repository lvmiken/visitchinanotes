## Start with one anchor goal per day

Good park days usually work better when each day centers on:

- one major scenic area
- realistic walking energy
- extra time for weather, queues, and slow movement

## Treat flexibility as part of the plan

In mountain destinations, a calmer plan is not wasted time. It is what gives you a better chance of actually enjoying the views instead of spending the whole day chasing the next transfer.
