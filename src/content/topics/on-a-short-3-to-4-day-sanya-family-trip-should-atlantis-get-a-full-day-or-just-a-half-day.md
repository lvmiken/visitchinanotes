On a short `3 to 4 day` family trip, Atlantis should usually be a **half day**.

That is the clean default.

A full day only makes sense when the family really wants Atlantis to be one of the trip's main events, not just one famous detour.

## When a full day is honest

Give Atlantis a full day when:

- `Aquaventure` is clearly the point
- the kids are strongly waterpark-driven
- the family is happy for one short-trip day to be almost entirely Atlantis-shaped

If those things are not true, a full day often ends up feeling oversized.

## Why half a day usually works better

Short Sanya trips still need:

- one good coast rhythm
- one easier recovery block
- time to enjoy the hotel base you actually chose

Half a day lets Atlantis stay memorable without swallowing those other things.

## The one question to ask

Ask this:

`On this short trip, is Atlantis one chapter or the chapter?`

If it is one chapter, keep it to half a day.

If it is the chapter, a full day can be honest.

## Editorial default

For many first-time families on a short `Sanya` stay:

- keep Atlantis to half a day
- expand to a full day only when the waterpark or full resort experience is clearly the point

That default keeps the short trip feeling edited instead of hijacked.
