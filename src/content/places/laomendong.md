`Laomendong` often is the part of Nanjing that makes the stop feel less stern.

## The short answer

It is usually worth it when:

- the day wants a softer old-city layer
- the evening is already leaning toward [Confucius Temple and the Qinhuai River](/places/confucius-temple-and-qinhuai-river/)
- you want balance after heavier historical sightseeing

## Laomendong vs Confucius Temple and Qinhuai

Choose `Laomendong` if:

- you want the calmer lead-in
- the evening should start softer

Choose [Confucius Temple and the Qinhuai River](/places/confucius-temple-and-qinhuai-river/) if:

- you only have one protected evening block
- you want the city's stronger classic night answer
