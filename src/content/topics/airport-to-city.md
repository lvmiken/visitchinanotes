Many first-time travelers plan around the flight arrival time and forget that the city only really begins once they have actually reached the hotel and settled in.

## Treat the transfer as part of the first day

The real arrival day includes:

- airport procedures
- getting into the city
- finding the hotel
- resetting energy after the flight

## Let the first hotel reduce stress

A calmer first base can make the first night, the first metro ride, and the first real sightseeing day much smoother.
