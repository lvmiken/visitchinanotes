## The short answer

Choose `Yulong River rafting` when:

- the trip wants a gentle countryside half day
- you already have enough bigger `Li River` identity elsewhere
- the group would enjoy sitting with scenery more than chasing another famous photo

Skip or demote it when:

- only one scenic half day can fit and the route still needs `Xingping`
- the whole region is too short
- the trip already is overloaded with paid scenic add-ons

## Why rafting is often the best Yulong version

The rafting version usually works because it keeps the Yulong experience honest:

- scenic
- easy
- countryside-led

That fits the best version of Yangshuo for many first-time visitors.

## When it beats one more town hour

It usually beats more `West Street` time when:

- the evening already is protected
- the trip still needs one real countryside answer
- the route wants the soft side of Yangshuo, not just the social side

## When Xingping still wins

Choose [Xingping Ancient Town: Go for the river edge, not the old-street fantasy](/places/xingping-ancient-town/) instead if:

- the route still needs the stronger iconic-river branch
- the `20 RMB` geography matters
- the scenic half day should feel more famous than soft
