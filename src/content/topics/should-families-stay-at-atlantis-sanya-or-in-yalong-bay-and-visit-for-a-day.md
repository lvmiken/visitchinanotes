For most first-time families, keep the hotel in `Yalong Bay` and use `Atlantis` as one outing.

That is the better default.

Sleeping at Atlantis should be the upgrade case, not the assumption.

## Why Yalong Bay usually wins

`Yalong Bay` wins because it keeps the stay easy:

- clearer beach rhythm
- less resort-brand gravity
- fewer decisions distorted by one giant attraction complex

If Atlantis is only one day in the plan, Yalong Bay usually gives the better overall family stay.

## When Atlantis as hotel is actually right

Atlantis becomes the stronger hotel when the family really wants:

- repeated attraction use
- easier midday room returns
- one self-contained resort chapter

That is not a small difference. It means the family is choosing Atlantis as a system, not just as a famous address.

## The deciding test

Ask this:

`Do we want Atlantis once, or do we want Atlantis again tomorrow?`

If the answer is once, choose `Yalong Bay`.

If the answer is again tomorrow, Atlantis gets much stronger.

## Editorial default

For many first-time families:

- sleep in `Yalong Bay`
- visit `Atlantis` for a day
- move the hotel to Atlantis only if repeated use is clearly central

That default keeps the family trip from becoming more expensive and more resort-led than it needed to be.
