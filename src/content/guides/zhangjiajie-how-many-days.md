Zhangjiajie is one of those places where the answer depends less on distance and more on how much flexibility the trip can afford.

## Choose two days if

- this is one stop in a wider route
- you mainly want one signature scenery experience
- the trip cannot give the mountains extra weather buffer

## Choose more time if

- this is a core reason for visiting China
- you want a slower nature rhythm
- you do not want one cloudy or tiring day to define the stop
