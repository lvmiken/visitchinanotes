In Chengdu, your hotel choice shapes the rhythm of the trip more than the distance between headline sights.

## Choose between convenience and atmosphere

- Pick convenience if you have a short stay.
- Pick atmosphere if the trip is more about strolling, tea houses, and evening food culture.

## Think about evenings

Some cities are planned around mornings. Chengdu often shines most in the evening, so a base that makes nighttime returns easy is worth paying for.
