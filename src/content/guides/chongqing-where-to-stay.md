In Chongqing, the hotel area shapes how tiring the city feels almost as much as the sightseeing plan does.

## Choose your base by movement and energy

- Pick the most convenient area if this is your first experience with the city's terrain.
- Pick a view-forward area if skyline atmosphere matters more than the simplest movement.
- Pick a food-oriented area if evenings are central to the trip.

## Keep evenings in the calculation

The right base should still feel easy after dark, when food streets, river views, and returning to the hotel all become part of the experience.
