This is one of the most useful Nanjing hotel decisions because the two areas solve different problems very clearly.

## Choose Xinjiekou if convenience comes first

Choose `Xinjiekou` if:

- you want the easiest central base
- the stay is short
- you do not want the evening district to dictate the whole hotel choice

## Choose Confucius Temple if atmosphere comes first

Choose `Confucius Temple` if:

- [Confucius Temple and the Qinhuai River](/places/confucius-temple-and-qinhuai-river/) are a major part of the trip
- you want a stronger old-city night feel
- the hotel should make the city feel memorable, not only efficient

## A practical rule

- choose `Xinjiekou` for the safer default
- choose `Confucius Temple` for the more atmospheric version
