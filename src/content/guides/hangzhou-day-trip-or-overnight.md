Hangzhou can work as either a fast scenic add-on or a more restorative stop between larger cities.

## Choose a day trip if

- your China itinerary is tight
- you mainly want a West Lake overview
- you already have a Shanghai base

## Choose an overnight stay if

- you want a slower travel day
- you care about sunrise or evening scenery
- you want room for tea-culture stops
