`Nanjing City Wall` often helps the city feel more physical and less abstract.

## The short answer

It is usually worth it when:

- you want one outdoor historical layer
- the city already has enough indoor history
- the route has enough time for a supporting half day

It is less worth forcing when:

- the trip already is overloaded with major history blocks
- the live choice really is between this and protecting the city's one good evening

## Nanjing City Wall vs Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum

Choose `Nanjing City Wall` if:

- you want a more flexible history layer
- the route does not want another big symbolic excursion

Choose [Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum](/places/sun-yat-sen-mausoleum/) if:

- the city still needs one stronger monumental anchor
