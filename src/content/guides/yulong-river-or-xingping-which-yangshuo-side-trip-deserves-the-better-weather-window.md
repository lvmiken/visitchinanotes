This is one of the best Yangshuo tradeoffs because both options are good.

They are just good in very different ways.

## Source check

This page was checked against current Guilin-region source material on **June 27, 2026**, including Visit Guilin's current [Yulong River page](https://visitguilin.org/things-to-do/yangshuo-attractions/yulong-river/), China Highlights' current [Xingping Ancient Town page](https://www.chinahighlights.com/yangshuo/attraction/xingping-anceint-town.htm), and TravelChinaGuide's current [Yellow Cloth Shoal page](https://www.travelchinaguide.com/attraction/guangxi/guilin/li_river/yellow-cloth-shoal.htm). I am mainly using those sources to keep the comparison honest: one side is the softer countryside branch, and the other is the stronger Li River icon branch.

## The short answer

Choose [Yulong River in Yangshuo: The calm countryside half-day that often beats one more town hour](/places/yulong-river/) if:

- the trip wants softness
- the group values ease over icon chasing
- Yangshuo already has enough classic river identity elsewhere

Choose [Xingping Ancient Town: Go for the river edge, not the old-street fantasy](/places/xingping-ancient-town/) if:

- the route still wants the stronger `Li River` image
- the `20 RMB` geography matters
- only one more iconic half day can fit

## Give the better weather to Xingping if the icon still matters

If your route still needs one stronger classic Guilin-region image, `Xingping` usually deserves the better visibility.

That is because the whole logic of the side trip is more image-dependent:

- river profile
- banknote landscape
- classic Li River identity

## Give the softer day to Yulong if the route already feels scenic enough

If the route already has enough big-image value, `Yulong River` often becomes the better emotional answer.

Why:

- it asks less from the day
- it often feels calmer
- it gives Yangshuo a more habitable countryside rhythm

## The simple decision rule

Use this rule:

- `Need the icon? Choose Xingping.`
- `Need the softness? Choose Yulong.`

That is usually cleaner than pretending both solve the same problem.
