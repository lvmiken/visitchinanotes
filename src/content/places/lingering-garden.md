`Lingering Garden` usually is not the garden that proves Suzhou belongs in the trip.

It is the garden that deepens Suzhou after that first decision already is secure.

## The short answer

It is usually worth it when:

- the stay is slower or longer
- you truly want a second serious garden
- the city already has room beyond [Humble Administrator's Garden](/places/humble-administrators-garden/)

It is less worth forcing when:

- Suzhou only has one packed day
- the live choice really is between another garden and one stronger evening or street block

## Lingering Garden vs Humble Administrator's Garden

Choose [Humble Administrator's Garden](/places/humble-administrators-garden/) if:

- you only have one garden slot
- the city still needs its first major classical anchor

Choose `Lingering Garden` if:

- you already know Suzhou is getting a second garden
- the trip style rewards depth more than breadth
